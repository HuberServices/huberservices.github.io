About Huber Tree Service

Huber Tree Service is your trusted partner for all your tree care needs. As a family-owned business based in Huntsville, Alabama, we take pride in providing top-quality services to our valued customers. Here’s what sets us apart:

Our Products

At Huber Tree Service, we specialize in tree care and maintenance. Our comprehensive range of products includes:

  1. Tree Trimming: Our certified arborists skillfully trim and shape trees to enhance their health and aesthetics.
  2. Tree Removal: When a tree poses a risk or needs removal, our team ensures safe and efficient removal.
  3. Stump Grinding: We eliminate unsightly stumps, allowing you to reclaim your outdoor space.
  4. Emergency Storm Damage Cleanup: When storms strike, count on us to restore order and safety.

Our Mission and Values

Our mission is simple: to provide exceptional tree care services while prioritizing safety, integrity, and customer satisfaction. We believe in:

  • Quality: We deliver the highest standard of workmanship and attention to detail.
  • Safety: Our team follows industry best practices to ensure safety during every project.
  • Environmental Stewardship: We respect nature and strive to minimize our impact on the environment.
  • Community: We’re committed to serving our local community and building lasting relationships.

Meet Our Founders

James Huber founded Huber Tree Service over two decades ago. His passion for trees and dedication to excellence laid the foundation for our company. Today, James continues to oversee operations and ensures that our values remain at the core of everything we do.

What Makes Us Different?

  1. Experience: With years of experience, we understand trees inside out. Our expertise allows us to handle any tree-related challenge.
  2. Certified Arborists: Our team includes certified arborists who bring knowledge and precision to every job.
  3. Customer-Centric Approach: We listen to our customers, tailor our services to their needs, and exceed expectations.
  4. State-of-the-Art Equipment: We invest in cutting-edge equipment to deliver efficient and effective solutions.

Contact Us

  • Email: customerservice@hubertree.com
  • Visit our website: HuberTree.com

Let us care for your trees with the expertise and passion they deserve! 🌳🌿
